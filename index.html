About me

Born and raised in three different continents to wonderlust parents, perhaps that is when I was infused with world view and the urge to travel. While growing up in different countries, I have learned four languages along the way: English, Mandarin, Cantonese, and Taiwanese.

Besides the human languages, I have taught myself more than half a dozen computer programming languages. Ever since tapping on the keyboard of my father's i286 computer in the 90s, I have been drawn to the leading edge of technology. I have built my own desktop computer, programmed my car's on-board diagnostic system, and wrote mobile applications to help with everyday life. All of which were accomplished by lots of self-learning.

The skills I have taught myself goes on to include music, photography, tinkering with various machines, and wilderness survival skills. To put my outdoor knowledge to good use, I became an executive of the University of Ottawa Outdoors Club for several years and lead numerous trips. Not only was I able to hone my skills as an event organizer, I was able to satisfy my need to travel, playing guitar by the campfire, and capture the memorable moments in the form of photography. Most importantly, enjoying time with friends and doing the things we love, isn't that what life is all about?
